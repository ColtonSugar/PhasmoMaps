Weather Monkey Paw Wishes

For all weather-related Monkey Paw wishes, you will become momentarily blinded while the weather undergoes a change. It reduces your sanity by 25%.

  • I wish for clear skies – The weather will change to clear.
  • I wish for rain – The weather will change to rainy.
  • I wish for snow – The weather will change to snow.
  • I wish for sunrise – The weather and lighting will change to sunrise.
  • I wish for fog – The weather will change to foggy.

Ghost Information Monkey Paw Wishes

  • I wish for knowledge – The Monkey Paw will offer you a hint about the ghost's identity in your Journal, crossing out incorrect evidence. Your hearing and sight will be impaired for the rest of the game, and a hunt will start.
  • I wish for Sanity/I wish to be sane – The Monkey Paw will increase your team's average sanity to 50%, but your sanity will drain faster. The ghost may also move to its favorite room.
  • I wish to see the ghost – A ghost event will trigger at the ghost's location. Your vision will be obscured with dark fog for 5 seconds, then a hunt will start. Your vision returns after the hunt ends.
  • I wish for activity – Doubles ghost activity for 2 minutes. However, the front door locks and the fuse box breaks for 2 minutes.

Hunt-Related Monkey Paw Wishes

  • I wish the ghost was trapped / I wish to trap the ghost – The ghost is locked in its favorite room for 1 minute. However, you are also locked in the room you are currently in. When the minute ends, a cursed hunt begins.
  • I wish to leave – The Monkey Paw will unlock all exit doors, even during a hunt. However, you will be frozen in place for 5 seconds and move slower for another 5 seconds. Your vision is also impaired.
  • I wish I was safe / I wish to be safe – Smashes all lights in your current room but opens a hiding spot. However, the ghost can now hear and detect equipment from any range.
  • I wish for life – Has a chance to revive a dead teammate, but another player (or you) might die. The chance of death increases by 50%.

Other Wishes

  • I wish for anything – Triggers a random wish with unknown effects.
